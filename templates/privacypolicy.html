Privacy Policy

Introduction

This website allows users to login with their Spotify accounts to manage playlists, including removing duplicate songs. Your privacy is important to us.

Information Collection

We collect information related to your Spotify account and playlists when you authorize our app.

Use of Information

The information collected is used to display your playlists and help you manage duplicates within them.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your information is not shared with any third-party services, except as required to functionally support the website's features.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses Spotify's API to access your playlists but does not use additional third-party services for data collection.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Any changes to our privacy policy will be posted on this page.
